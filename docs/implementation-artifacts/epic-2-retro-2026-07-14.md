## What went well

- **The centerpiece landed** (Danilo): "the battle animations and the new board style and positioning." The procedural-heritage bet — iso illusion in BOARD geometry, flat billboard sprites, zero custom art — paid off exactly as specced.
- **Process felt good** (Danilo): "what we proposed to do went great, less frustration this Epic." The spec → story → dev → adversarial review → device-acceptance loop held through a full visual-layer rewrite.
- **Executable guards became the house signature**: license gate (2.1) → rules-doc drift guard (2.4, pins HP/actions/RPS/AGI-order/misfire% to BALANCE). Player-facing content that cannot silently lie.
- **Architecture invariants survived contact**: AD-2 purity through the whole animation rewrite; AD-8's gateway shipped clean; the one sanctioned AD-2 exception (status-icon lifecycle) documented + deferred properly.
- **A repeated-mistake pattern was BROKEN mid-epic**: the singleton-scene trap (2.2 review finding) was memorized, pre-empted in 2.3's design, clean by 2.4.

## Challenges / lessons

- **Phaser 4 fought empirically, three times**: global `pixelArt` wrecked crispText on device; `add.polygon` rendered quads as triangles; `GeometryMask` silently failed to clip. All caught by screenshot, never by reasoning — "empirical over reasoned" is now doctrine (all three memorized).
- **The font ceiling**: text softness was precisely diagnosed (canvas backing fixed at 360×640, DPR-invariant; `?textres` mathematically can't help) and deferred with candidate fixes. Danilo: "I'll survive for now." Fix is story-sized + NFR1-gated.
- **The bottleneck moved to game balance** (Danilo: "I got distracted by the fact the battle is unbalanced"): good presentation exposed the rules. Wish-cluster logged with impact analysis: mage row-blast too strong (OB64 gates area damage behind Archmage), no target-selection tactics (attack weakest/strongest/autonomous), critical hits; plus the earlier archer-vs-casters FR14 item.
- Recurring review themes: tap-target/floor slips (36px vs 44px, 14px vs 15px), timer-restart classes (holding, alternating speed taps), positional-parsing fragility — all caught in review, none shipped.

## Significant discovery for planning (⚠️ input to the correct-course)

**Balance pass × Epic 3 sequencing:** AD-8 allows replay only when a stored `balanceVersion` matches the current one. If the balance pass lands AFTER Epic 3 ships history/replay, players' first saved battles become non-replayable. Rebalance-before-history avoids that entirely. The correct-course must decide the order explicitly.

Epic 3 dependencies are otherwise unusually well-fed: storage gateway exists (2.3), determinism golden-tested since epic 1, Battle presentation complete for replay, `vite-plugin-pwa` installed (unconfigured) for 3.3, and the deferred formal fps check + text-ceiling fix both point at 3.4.
